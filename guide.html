Culture & Heritage

Eldon House
📍 481 Ridout St N
London's oldest house (1834). Donation. Thu-Sun 12-5pm. Garden picnic legal.
Museum London
📍 421 Ridout St N
Free, on Forks of the Thames. Paul Peel's "After the Bath" is the quiet draw.
Banting House
📍 442 Adelaide St N
The room where insulin was conceived. Flame of Hope outside has burned since 1989.
Forest City Gallery
📍 258 Richmond St
Artist-run since 1973. Opening nights are free with cheap red wine.
Michael Gibson Gallery
📍 157 Carling St
Commercial gallery, contemporary Canadian. Quiet, cerebral.
Hume Cronyn Observatory
📍 Western U campus, 1151 Richmond St
Free public nights. A grad student aims a 100-year-old refractor at Saturn for you.

Within 2 Hours

Day Trips

Ranked for Saturday April 25, 2026, factoring season and what's actually open.

1. Stratford
55-65 min
Festival opens April 21 — opening weekend energy. Avon swans, Revival House (70 Brunswick St), Mercer Hall, Bradshaws. Capital-D Date.
2. Niagara-on-the-Lake
2hr-2hr15
Shaw Festival running. Funny Girl just opened. Wineries pre-tourist quiet. Cherry blossoms emerging. Best as overnight getaway.
3. Bayfield
60-70 min
Lake Huron sunset signature. Pioneer Park clifftop, Little Inn (1832), Black Dog Pub. Year-round village.
4. Elora + St. Jacobs
75-90 min
Sat market 7am-3:30pm at St. Jacobs (878 Weber St N). Then Elora Gorge + Mill Hotel dinner. Spring runoff = peak gorge drama.
5. Port Stanley
40-45 min
Closest beach town. Empty April beach, Kettle Creek Inn (216 Joseph St), antique row, lighthouse pier.
St. Marys
30-40 min
Limestone "Stonetown." Trestle bridge walk, Broken Rail Brewery. Westover Inn restaurant reopens May 7.
Grand Bend / Pinery
65-75 min
Pinery dunes empty in April. Town shuttered. Aux Sables Inn restaurant if you want to eat.
Quai du Vin Winery (Sparta)
25-35 min
45811 Fruit Ridge Line, St. Thomas. Family-run since 1972. Vineyard + Sparta Tea Room.
St. Thomas / Talbotville
20-25 min
Jumbo statue, Railway City Brewing, Ember Bistro inside CASO 1873 train station. Cheap quirky evening.
