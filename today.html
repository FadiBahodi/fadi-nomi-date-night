PLAN A · The Headliner

Market morning, slow afternoon, vintage horror, late dinner

A perfect Saturday in London with one foot in nostalgia. You sleep in, hit the Western Fair Market for breakfast and people-watching, walk the gardens, take a couple of slow hours, then a campy double-feature horror night at the Hyland followed by late drinks and a small plate. Puppy at home most of the day, garden walk in the late afternoon brings him into the loop.

Eldon House gardens + Forks of the Thames

📍 481 Ridout St N, London ON · 7 min drive · 12pm–5pm
